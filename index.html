Introduction

Introduction

Visual Media Promotion (Introduction)

Did you decide to travel Canada? It is an amazing choice. Canada is one of the most beautiful countries in the world. As you get to know this country, you will begin to fall in love with it. Canada has a unique nature, friendly people, and multi culture. That is why Canada is called "The Happiest Country in the world".



Natural Wonderland

Topic #1

Natural Wonderland

Considered one of the most beautiful countries in the world for the beauty of its nature, Canada admires its endless forests, blue lakes and rivers, breathtaking waterfalls, majestic mountains, natural treasures, and wildlife those who travel here. Surrounded by oceans on three sides, this huge country has a different beauty in every season. This giant country, which has a different beauty in every corner, offers unlimited opportunities and experiences to visitors. If you have already bought your tickets, you must travel Niagara Falls (Ontario) where Canada's most famous place, Banff National Park is located in the heart of the majestic Rocky Mountains in the province of Alberta, Old Quebec city where is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, Whistler ski resort in Vancouver, Polar bears of Churchill in Northern Manitoba, Vancouver Island in British Columbia, Bay of Fundy in New Brunswick and Nova Scotia, Gros Morne National Park in Newfoundland, Yukon and so on. The richness and beauty of Canadian nature cannot be finished by counting.



Diversity

Topic #2

Diversity

One of the reasons you travel to Canada is that it has a multi-culture. In this country, people have every right, regardless of language, religion, ethnicity, culture, nationality, gender, physical appearance and dress. No one can judge you according to any of your characteristics. Everyone is equal and free. By traveling here, you can get acquainted with different nations and their civilizations and cuisines. Everyone in this country is free to promote their own culture.



Family-Friendly

Topic #3

Family-Friendly

If you are traveling to Canada with your family and children, you will not be mistaken, because Canada ranks first ten among the countries in the world for protecting family values. Many call Canada a country for children. As you travel, you can meet families who are having fun with their children in parks and forests. There are also many special activities for families wherever you travel within this country to have fun together. People here are always smiling and friendly, so people don't feel like strangers here.



Outdoor Activities

Topic #4

Outdoor Activities

In Canada, people love to play sports and have different adventures. And people do it in their daily lives. When you travel in summer, you can enjoy by doing kayaking, sky diving, swimming, zipline, kite boarding, riding bike, hiking, camping, picking and water sports and many other natural activities. In the winter months, you can find skiing, snowboarding, dogsled adventure, snowmobiling, and many other activities. This country, where the choices are wide and inexhaustible, will make your trip unforgettable.



Events And Festivals

Topic #5

Events And Festivals

No matter what season of the year you travel to, you can participate in any festival or event in Canada. Given that Canadians love to have fun, the abundance of festivals and holidays here should come as no surprise. Especially in the summer months, there is a festival almost every weekend. Tulip festival, Bubble festivals, Food festivals, Music and Comedy, Film, Painting and Art festivals will give you irreplaceable experiences. Don't leave Canada without seeing Thanksgiving holiday and pumpkin festivals in the fall, which is the most wonderful autumn in the world. Or when you want to celebrate a real Christmas holiday, Canada is the best choice for you. Lighting festivals held in different provinces, and Christmas Markets will amaze you. In addition, in Canada, where the winter season is harsh, people also enjoy the cold of winter. The winter festival, which is held every year, especially in the province of Quebec, opens a wide door for those who love winter activities. In any season of the year, you can enjoy the countless festivals mentioned here.

Conclusion
